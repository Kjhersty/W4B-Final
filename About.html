Velaris Academy

Velaris Academy is a safe place to learn to hone and enhance your special abilities. You will be placed into specific house pertaining to your ability: Earth, Fire, Air, Water. Developing new abilities and enhancing the ones you already have will be the main study along with other academic studies such as: History, Potions, Divination, Herbology and fighting/defenses classes. There are different levels of studies: Advanced, Strategic, and Benchmark, these determine the pace of study to make sure you are learning at the students pace. After taking the placement test, you will recieve a letter in regards to what house you were placed in and books and items you are responsible in retrieving to bring to the academy.
