Velaris Academy

Velaris Academy is a safe place to learn to hone and enhance your special abilities. You will be placed into specific house pertaining to your ability: Earth, Fire, Air, Water Night and Dawn Court. Developing new abilities and enhancing the ones you already have will be the main study along with other academic studies such as: History, Potions, Divination, Herbology and fighting/defenses classes. There are different levels of studies: Advanced, Strategic, and Benchmark, these determine the pace of study to make sure you are learning at the students pace.

So, you've just been sorted into your Elements Court. It's an exciting time in any new pupils' life, but it naturally comes with a lot of questions. What does it mean to be sorted into a paticular house? What does it say about your personality? Your Strength and weaknesses And most importantly, your house is the best one of them all, right?

Air Court: Founder: Helion Head of House: Cassian Neander House Colors: Purple and Silver You might belong in Air House, where dwell the heritage heart, they are cunning, witty, and resourceful.

Water Court: Founder: Nesta Archeron Head of House: Elain Archeron House Colors: Blue and Black You might belong in Water House, where dwell the ambitious heart, they are wise, patient and chivalrous.

Night Court: Founder: Rhysand Meduso Head of House: Azriel House Colors: Silver and Black You might belong to Night Court, where dwell the determined heart, they are logical, dedicated and have a high tolerance.

Dawn Court: Founder: Feyre Archeron Head of House: Beron Vanserra House Colors: Maroon and Black You might belong to Dawn Court, where dwell the clever heart, they are daring, courageous, and highly accomplished.

Congratulations on being sorted into your Courts and make sure to take the time to explore the community surroundings of whichever house you have been placed into.

After taking the placement test, you will recieve a letter in regards to what house you were placed in and books and items you are responsible in retrieving to bring to the academy.
